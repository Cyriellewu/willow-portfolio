Field note

Useful data is not the same as big data.

Nine million fault-log rows did not become useful until I stopped asking "what can I predict?" and started asking "what can a maintenance team actually act on?" The breakthrough was a conversation with the stakeholder, not a bigger model.

Project reflection

A calendar remembers plans, not context.

Most productivity tools preserve what you intended to do. They discard what was happening around it — the walk, the call, the mood of the afternoon. Evidence of Life was built around the opposite assumption: capture the life first, attach the plan later.

Field note

The model is not allowed to draw the symbols.

In Oracle OS, symbolic material — hexagrams, cards, pillars — comes from deterministic code engines. The LLM is constrained to interpretation only. It cannot invent a cast, predict an outcome, or manufacture certainty. The boundary between system and story is explicit.

Project reflection

Prettier does not necessarily mean more trustworthy.

Enhancing a palm-vein image can make it visually smoother while destroying the branching structure a biometric system relies on. I evaluated enhancement by similarity to a clean reference, not by how good the output looked. Visual polish is not fidelity.
