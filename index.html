Tiny field notes

A few beliefs I bring to the whiteboard.

A

Reliability is an experience.

A graceful failure message is still product work. So is making sure the right conversation remains the right conversation under load.

B

Metrics need a reason.

I like measuring carefully — then asking whether the number represents the problem a person was actually trying to solve.

C

Serious work can still be joyful.

The best teams make room for both a rigorous evaluation plan and the occasional very specific sticker.
