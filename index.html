Research / ACML 2025 / Published as You WU

"Predict failure" was too vague. I rewrote the question.

Vehicle fault logs did not become useful just because there were millions of them. I reframed the work around emergency-fee risk, repair cost, and fault categories - decisions a maintenance team could actually act on.

The interesting part - the hard work was asking what the data could honestly support before optimizing a model.
